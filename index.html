About

The Intro...

I am an aspiring Systems Administrator with a passion for building clear and maintainable digital experiences. I have a background in Helpdesk support and designing and maintaining my home-lab and this website! I have recently been spending my time learning about and developing applications utilizing Agentic coding techniques, such as Codex and Claude this has expanded my understanding of the CI/CD pipeline and exposed me to different approaches to problem solving. I think the biggest benefit of using LLM's is the increase in velocity it provides to teams that know how to use it. I am interested in seeing how the future looks in a world where up and coming developers need to learn to code but may not need to be experts in it. I am also interested in the security implications of LLM's and how they will change the landscape of cybersecurity. I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field and sharing my journey with others.
